## NOT-DO risks

1. Do not target `palcalculator` alone as the SEO keyword. It is too ambiguous and not clearly indexed as a demand term.
2. Do not launch a thin clone of a parent-pair breeding calculator. The SERP already has Palworld.gg, Palpedia, Pal Routes, PalworldBreedCalculator.com, Game8, PalDB, Pal-Breed, and others.
3. Do not use copyrighted game assets, logos, or official-looking branding without permission. Use text/data-first UI and a clear fan-site disclaimer.
4. Do not claim to be official. Use тАЬfan-made Palworld calculatorтАЭ positioning.
5. Do not publish stale breeding data. Palworld 1.0 freshness is central; show update date and data source policy.
6. Do not overpromise passive inheritance or IV certainty where RNG/rounding creates uncertainty.
7. Do not upload/process save files server-side unless there is a real privacy/security implementation. Prefer browser-local parsing if supported.
8. Do not bury the calculator under long SEO copy. Tool input must be above the fold.
9. Do not choose generic тАЬPal calculatorтАЭ copy without Palworld context; it collides with physical activity level and other PAL meanings.
10. Do not depend on a single competitor scrape as data source; keep a documented data pipeline/update method.
